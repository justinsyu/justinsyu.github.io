About Me

I am a life sciences researcher with a passion for working with others to solve unique and difficult problems, finding ways to leverage data that challenge the norm, and developing products that directly meet the needs of stakeholders while generating insights for future research.


My current goals are to work in health technology and develop products that will help clinicians provide better care, people live better lives, lower healthcare costs, and improve outcomes in all healthcare settings.
